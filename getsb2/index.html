getsb2makes sb2 files from project IDs.

Just put a project ID or URL after the /.

  • getsb2.herokuapp.com/15832807
  • getsb2.herokuapp.com/http://scratch.mit.edu/projects/15832807/

You can also add .zip to download the contents of the sb2 as a zip file, .txt to export a project summary, and .json to get just the JSON data.

  • getsb2.herokuapp.com/15832807.zip
  • getsb2.herokuapp.com/15832807.txt
  • getsb2.herokuapp.com/15832807.json
